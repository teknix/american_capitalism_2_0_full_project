# American Capitalism 2.0 — For a Strong America

This repository hosts the living thesis for **American Capitalism 2.0**: a
pragmatic framework to rebuild a broad American middle class, empower small
business, and keep markets free, fair, and competitive.

> **Strong Markets. Strong Middle Class. Strong America.**

## Contents

- `docs/thesis.md` — latest working thesis (living document)
- `releases/thesis_v1.0.md` and `releases/american_capitalism_2_0_thesis.odt` — published release
- `branding/usage-guidelines.md` — logo/name usage rules (restricted license)
- `CONTRIBUTING.md` — how to propose changes; no anonymous edits
- `CODEOWNERS` — ownership for `/docs` and `/thesis`

## Pillars (8)

1. Wage–Productivity Partnership  
2. Anti-Monopoly Revival  
3. Automation Dividend  
4. American Health Guarantee  
5. Reduced Workweek Transition  
6. Skilled Trades & Reshoring Revival  
7. Small Business First  
8. Civic Service & National Resilience
