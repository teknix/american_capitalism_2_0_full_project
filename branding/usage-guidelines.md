# American Capitalism 2.0 Branding Guidelines

The name **“American Capitalism 2.0”** and all associated logos, seals, and
graphic marks are protected under a restricted license.

They may not be:

- reproduced,
- modified,
- distributed,
- or used for commercial, political, or promotional purposes

without explicit written permission from the author.

Political entities may publicly state that they align with the principles of
American Capitalism 2.0, but may not use the logo, seal, or other branding in
any campaign materials, merchandise, policy documents, or media appearances
without prior written permission.
